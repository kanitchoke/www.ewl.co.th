Welcome to East-West Logistics
East-West are pioneers in the Thai forwarding industry. Established in 1977 to provide a personalised, professional forwarding service to Thailand's growing number of importers and exporters, East-West Logistics has since expanded its operations to include sea freight, licensed customers brokerage, packings/removals, warehousing, chartering and project cargo.
